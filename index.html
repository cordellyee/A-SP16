What Ascend Can Do for You

Our mission is to be the premier professional organization dedicated to enabling its members, business partners and the community to leverage the leadership and global business potential of Pan-Asians.

Dedication

We're dedicated to our members and committed to offering our members professional development programs and unparalleled access to key decision makers and senior executives across industries.

Development

Access to world-class leadership and professional development programs, unique Pan-Asian leadership networks, and opportunities to develop your leadership skills and influence in the community.

Access

Our goal is to help each member reach their career aspirations and maximize their leadership potential while bringing increased value to their organizations. Network with our corporate sponsors directly.

Why Join

We're a family of over 100 students, but we cater to each individual's needs through mentorship and workshops.

Mentor-Mentee System

An experienced Executive Board member will help you with anything ranging from academics to interview preperation

Workshops

We host workshops catered to the needs of our members so that they continue to get value, month after month

Corporate Sponsors

We work with over two dozen corporate sponsors who look to hire at Ascend events. They also visit and host info sessions for our members, so that you can get advice from the recruiters themselves.
